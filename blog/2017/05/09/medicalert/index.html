Why MedicAlert

MedicAlert's been speaking for Canadians in emergencies for over 50 years — saving countless lives.  If you have a medical condition, that can put you at-risk, MedicAlert can help.

 

The MedicAlert Difference

MedicAlert's your voice in an emergency.  Depend upon MedicAlert to deliver the right information, at the right time, and in the right place.

The MedicAlert 24-hour emergency hotline's your connection.

  • To paramedics — providing your full MedicAlert medical history — in just 5 seconds.
  • To your loved ones — immediately, after the hotline's been called — letting them know that you're being taken care of.

The right information's critical.  So, every MedicAlert subscriber's medical record's reviewed by their trained medical specialists — prioritizing, the most critical medical information for emergency responders.
