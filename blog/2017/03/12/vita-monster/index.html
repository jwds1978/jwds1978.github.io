The docor's going to complete another Assistive Devices Program (ADP) form for me in order to get a powered mobility scooter.  The program will cover 100% of its cost, as they did for my orthosis.

I'm not sure as to what specific brands and/or models are approved and/or covered by the ADP, but this one would be nice to have though…  
 
Given that I like to do outdoor stuffs (i.e. camping, fishing, hunting, etc.), one such as that in the video above would be ideal for me.  But, I'll need to talk to an ADP vendor though and see what they have that's ADP-approved.
